30-Day Return Policy

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all purchases. If you're not completely happy with your order, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. The 30-day window starts from the date you receive your package, not the date of purchase.

What Qualifies for a Return

To be eligible for a return, items must be:

  • Unopened and unused
  • In their original packaging with all included accessories
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Accompanied by a valid order number

Please note that items purchased during promotional sales or as part of a bundle may have modified return terms. Check your order confirmation email for details.

Hygiene Policy

For hygiene and safety reasons, opened intimate products cannot be returned or exchanged. We take this policy seriously to protect the health and safety of all our customers. However, all opened items are fully covered by our 1-year product warranty, so if anything goes wrong, we've got you covered.

1-Year Product Warranty

Every Nancy product comes with a 1-year warranty against manufacturing defects, starting from the date of purchase. This warranty covers:

  • Motor or vibration malfunctions
  • Charging or battery defects
  • Material defects (peeling, discoloration, or degradation)
  • Button or control failures

The warranty does not cover damage from misuse, unauthorized modifications, or normal wear and tear. If your product has a manufacturing defect, we'll replace it or issue a full refund at our discretion.

How to Contact Us

For all returns, exchanges, and warranty claims, email us at support@hellonancy.co with your order number and a brief description of the issue. Our team typically responds within 24 hours and will guide you through the process.
