Shipping Info

Fast, free, and always discreet.

We ship to 50+ countries worldwide from our fulfillment centers in Hong Kong and the United States. Whether you're across town or across the globe, we'll get your Nancy to you as quickly and discreetly as possible.

Discreet Packaging

Your privacy matters to us. Every Nancy order is shipped in a plain, unmarked box with no logos, branding, or product descriptions on the outside. The return address uses a generic company name, so nothing on the package indicates what's inside.

Your billing statement will also show a generic merchant name rather than "Hello Nancy" or any product-related description.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package in real-time. Tracking information usually becomes active within 24-48 hours of shipment. If you haven't received your tracking email, check your spam folder or contact us at support@hellonancy.co.

Customs & Duties

For international orders, we handle all customs declarations on our end. However, the buyer is responsible for any import duties, taxes, or customs fees that may be charged by your country's customs authority. These charges vary by country and are not included in our shipping costs.

We recommend checking with your local customs office if you're unsure about potential charges for your region. The package description on customs forms will be listed generically as "personal care device" for your privacy.
