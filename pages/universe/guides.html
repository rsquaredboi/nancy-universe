Not sure where to start?

Choosing your first pleasure toy can feel overwhelming. There are so many options, so many shapes, so many features. We get it. That's why we designed the Nancy lineup to be approachable, intuitive, and impossible to get wrong.

Which Nancy is right for me?

If you're brand new to pleasure toys, we almost always recommend starting with Lem. It's our original character and it's designed to be gentle enough for first-timers while powerful enough for experienced users. The air-pulse technology means no direct contact is required, which makes it a great entry point for people who aren't sure what they like yet.

If you already know you prefer internal stimulation, Berri is your pick. If you want something versatile that works for both solo and couples play, Avo offers the most flexibility. Pixie is for people who want something compact and travel-friendly. And Lolly is for anyone who wants the most playful, feature-packed experience in the lineup.

What about materials and safety?

Every Nancy product is made from medical-grade silicone, which is the safest and most hygienic material available for pleasure products. It's non-porous, hypoallergenic, and easy to clean with warm water and mild soap. All of our products are IPX7 waterproof rated, meaning they're fully submersible and safe for use in the bath or shower.

How do I talk to my partner about this?

Honestly? Just be honest. Most partners are more curious than you think. The key is framing it as something you want to explore together, not something that replaces anything. Our Couples' Guide goes deeper into conversation starters, but the short version is: lead with curiosity, not pressure. And maybe share a link to Avo, which was literally designed with couples in mind.

The best guide is your own curiosity. Start there, and the rest follows.
