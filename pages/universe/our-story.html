Our Story

From a Hong Kong apartment to 50+ countries.

The Origin

We started Nancy because we were tired of pleasure products that looked like they belonged in a medical catalog. Every option was either clinical and cold, or trying so hard to be "discreet" that it had no personality at all. We thought: what if a pleasure brand felt more like your favorite beauty brand? What if it was something you actually wanted to display on your shelf?

In a small apartment in Hong Kong, we started sketching. Not product specs or engineering diagrams, but characters. Personalities. A world where each product had a name, a vibe, a reason to exist beyond function. That's how the Nancy Universe was born.

The Mission

Pleasure should be joyful. Pleasure should be colorful. Pleasure should be shame-free. These aren't just taglines for us, they're the foundation of every decision we make. From the pastel packaging to the character names to the way we talk about our products in public, everything is designed to normalize something that never should have been stigmatized.

We don't whisper about what we do. We celebrate it.

Nancy exists to make the world a little more open, a little more honest, and a lot more fun. We believe that when people feel free to explore their own pleasure without guilt, everything else in life gets better too.

The Team

Nancy is designed by an all-female product team based in Hong Kong. From industrial design to packaging to brand experience, every touchpoint is shaped by women who understand what it means to feel overlooked by an industry that claims to be "for women." We don't outsource our design thinking. We live it.

Our team brings together backgrounds in consumer electronics, beauty, fashion, and wellness. That cross-pollination is what makes Nancy feel different. We approach pleasure products with the same rigor and attention to detail that goes into a Dyson or an Apple product, but with the warmth and personality of your favorite indie beauty brand.

The Name

Nancy is a character, not a person. She represents boldness, honesty, and the refusal to be embarrassed about what you want. We chose the name because it's familiar, friendly, and a little bit cheeky. Nancy is the friend who says the thing everyone is thinking. She's the one who texts you the link. She's the energy we wanted this whole brand to embody.

When you buy a Nancy product, you're not just buying a toy. You're joining a universe of characters, stories, and people who believe that pleasure is worth celebrating out loud.

The Journey

2023
Founded in Hong Kong
Nancy is born in a small apartment. First prototypes of Lem are hand-sculpted. The mission is set: make pleasure joyful, colorful, and shame-free.
Early 2024
Lem Launches
Our first character hits the market. 10,000 units sold in the first 3 months. The pastel lemon that started it all becomes a TikTok sensation overnight.
Late 2024
10K Units Sold
Lem reaches customers in 20+ countries. Word of mouth becomes our strongest marketing channel. The Fruit Club community begins to form.
Early 2025
Berri + Avo Join the Universe
Two new characters launch to massive demand. Berri brings gentle precision. Avo brings luxurious calm. The collection grows from one toy to a full lineup.
Mid 2025
50+ Countries, 1M TikTok Views
Nancy goes global. Our TikTok content crosses 1 million views. Partnerships with wellness creators bring the brand to new audiences worldwide.
2026
Fashion Line, Pixie + Lolly, Community 100K
The universe expands beyond toys. Fashion drops, plushies, limited editions. Pixie and Lolly launch. The Fruit Club community crosses 100,000 members.
