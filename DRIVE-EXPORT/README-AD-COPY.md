## Copy Angles Used

1. **Social Proof** — "500K+ sold", "15K+ reviews", ratings
2. **Gift** — "Gift-ready in one click", custom box, ships tomorrow
3. **Scarcity** — "Small batches of 500", waitlist, limited production
4. **Emotional** — "Designed to make you smile", serotonin, feel-good
5. **Personality** — Character-driven copy matching each plushie's vibe
6. **Quality** — Materials, craftsmanship, medical-grade, waterproof
7. **First-Time** — Beginner-friendly, start gentle, explore
8. **Collection** — 120+ plushies, waves, "good luck choosing"

## How to Upload to Meta Ads Manager

1. Go to **Ads Manager** → **Campaigns** → **Create**
2. Choose campaign objective (Traffic / Conversions)
3. At ad level, click **"Create Multiple Ads"** or use the **bulk import** option
4. Upload `META-BULK-UPLOAD.csv`
5. Upload matching images from the `DRIVE-EXPORT/` folders
6. Review and publish
